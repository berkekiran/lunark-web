NFT management will enable browsing, buying, selling, and transferring digital collectibles across major marketplaces through natural language. The assistant will handle marketplace integrations, price lookups, and transaction execution.

<Callout type="warn" title="Coming Soon">
This feature is currently in development.
</Callout>

## Overview

Planned features include collection discovery, floor price tracking, marketplace purchases on OpenSea and Blur, listing management, and wallet-to-wallet NFT transfers. You'll be able to manage your entire NFT portfolio through conversational commands.

## Planned Features

**Discovery**: Browse collections, view rarity traits, and track floor prices across OpenSea, Blur, and other marketplaces.

**Trading**: Buy NFTs at floor or make offers. List your NFTs for sale with custom pricing.

**Management**: View your collection, transfer NFTs to other wallets, and track portfolio value.

## Example Commands

```
"Show floor price for Bored Apes"
"Buy the cheapest Pudgy Penguin"
"List my CryptoPunk for 50 ETH"
"Transfer NFT #1234 to alice"
```
